What is your long term goal or career pathway?

The current trajectory in terms of career is for me about the quality of life.
I don't believe in the term work/life balance, It's just life. I was sold the lie, that money was the answer. So I tried to be an analyst. And was one...for around 10 years. A couple years ago, I graduated from acting school. My career path moving forward is about quality of life. I've always loved and had enjoyment in the arts. And this is the area of thinking I am leaning into . With an eye always on creative outlets, and this new skillset I see as enabling my creativity, the obvious blending of art and tech is on my mind. Combining those interests, from music, theatre, movement, film, and other interests like traveling and experiencing other cultures, I am excited at the imagery I am creating and manifesting for myself and whanau.

With this, recognising small additions and learnings, made consistent, will create sustained benefits and internal growth.
