Talking about html, CSS and Javascript. What springs to mind for me, is if we have a look at the common car. We can envisage a base level, made up of many different bits and parts, attached to our chassis. This is our Html level. Html is like our foundation: the chassis, the windows, the engine, and all the bits and parts that make a car a car.

The next level to look at revolves around what we look at! How our car looks and feels on the outside and on the inside!!! The colour of the whole and of each part, the style, textures inside and outside the car, add to the whole experience of the cars aesthetic.
This is our CSS level
A new set of rims, a roof rack, just a light touch of style added to our car. But can make our experience in the car very different. So far we have all of the bits and pieces to make this car work and look good doing it.

Then we come to our connector, activator and/or smarts. The advances in car mechanics and technology. Taking our basic mechanical setup and applying intelligent thinking to it.
