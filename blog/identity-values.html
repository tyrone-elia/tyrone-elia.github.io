Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make

Discovering behaviour about individuals I held as friends. The behaviours demonstrated by these individuals go against values I hold. The decision was a simple, but very hard one to make. I now don't interact with these people, which is sad but needed. I cannot condone certain actions of people, and endevour to do better and speak up, when I see or feel instances where action is required.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity

My culture... What is 'my' culture? New Zealand culture? Aotearoa?? Pacific Islander??
A good question really, and a somewhat triggering question.
Born in Auckland, New Zealand. My mother, a 7th gen New Zealander with roots going back to early settlers of Poverty Bay. My father, a 1st gen New Zealander from Kūki 'Āirani (The Cook Islands).
The reclamation of culture, identity or lack of is part of my influences and values to-date.

Share an example from an experience

2 years ago I formed a band. This band was made up of actors. They had not really been within the band environment before and this was a new setting for them. Although their musical ability was still at a high level. The tension I discovered recently, was two fold… in one way, these actors have always had the privilege to take up space and time for themselves to focus on what they need personally. Growing up… I was not allowed to do this. My childhood was more about being shushed and invisible. These are really big learnings for me. I have recognised this tension was both my inability to take up others time, and my reactions to others taking up everyones time. These have been difficult to accept, but I feel are good learnings for me moving forward.
